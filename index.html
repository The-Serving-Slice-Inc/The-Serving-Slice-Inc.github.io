Mission Statement


Difficult times require bold actions. The Serving Slice Initiative aspires to collaborate with essential food services across communities in order to provide for those most affected by the COVID-19 pandemic. We accept donations from local restaurants, cafes, and food chains and distribute them to groups in the most need such as senior centers, homeless shelters, and hospitals, free of transportation cost. With every meal packaged and slice of the pie cut, we seek to bring hope to the community in a time when we need it the most.
